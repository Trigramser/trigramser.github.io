PASSAGE 1: the most impressive trip


Travel Topic: Dali and Lijiang

1. What was the most impressive trip you have ever had?
The most impressive trip I have ever had was my trip to Dali and Lijiang in Yunnan. It was one of the most unforgettable journeys in my life because the scenery was beautiful, the atmosphere was peaceful, and the local culture was very unique.

2. Where did you go? When did you go there? How did you get there? Who did you go with? How long did you stay there?
I went to Dali and Lijiang during my vacation. I traveled there with my friends. We went to Yunnan by plane, and then traveled between Dali and Lijiang by train. We stayed there for several days.

3. What was the weather like there?
The weather there was very pleasant. It was cool and comfortable, and the air was fresh. The climate was perfect for traveling, walking around, and enjoying the scenery.

4. What did you do there? What did you see there?
In Dali, I visited Erhai Lake and Dali Old Town. I enjoyed walking around the old town and spending time near the lake. Dali is so peaceful, especially around Erhai Lake, where I rented a bike and explored the scenery. In Lijiang, I visited Lijiang Old Town and Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. I saw traditional wooden houses, stone streets, small canals, and beautiful mountain views.

5. What was the scenery like?
The scenery was absolutely breathtaking. In Dali, Erhai Lake was calm and beautiful, with mountains in the background. In Lijiang, the old town was charming, and Jade Dragon Snow Mountain was magnificent. The whole trip felt like walking through a painting.

6. What was special about the local culture and people?
What impressed me most was the local culture. In Dali, I learned a little about the Bai ethnic culture. In Lijiang I visited the Mu Residence in Lijiang Old Town and learned a lot about its rich history and cultural background, which made the trip even more enjoyable.

7. What local food did you try?
I tried some local food in Yunnan, and I really enjoyed it. The food was fresh, flavorful, and quite different from what I usually eat. Tasting local dishes was one of the most interesting parts of the trip.

8. Did anything interesting or unexpected happen during the trip?
Yes, there were some interesting moments during the trip. While walking through the old towns, I found some beautiful little streets and small local shops by accident. Those unexpected discoveries made the trip even more memorable.

9. What was your favorite part of the trip?
My favorite part of the trip was visiting Erhai Lake and Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. Erhai Lake made me feel peaceful and relaxed, while Jade Dragon Snow Mountain amazed me with its grand and breathtaking beauty.

10. Why was this trip so memorable for you?
This trip was so memorable because it was not only beautiful, but also meaningful. I enjoyed the amazing scenery, the peaceful atmosphere, and the unique ethnic culture. It gave me many wonderful memories.

11. How did you feel during and after the trip?
During the trip, I felt excited, relaxed, and happy. After the trip, I felt refreshed and satisfied. Even now, when I think about it, I still feel warm and peaceful inside.

12. Would you like to go there again? Why?
Yes, I would definitely like to go there again because there are still many places I want to explore. I also want to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and beautiful scenery one more time.

13. Would you recommend this place to other people?
Yes, I would definitely recommend Dali and Lijiang to other people. They are great places for people who love nature, traditional culture, and a slower pace of life. I think they are wonderful travel destinations.
